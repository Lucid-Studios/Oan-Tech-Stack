# CME PLACEMENT WITHHELD LAW

## Purpose

This note makes `CmePlacementWithheld` explicit `V1.2.1` law for governing
`CME` placement.

It exists to keep four things distinct:

- prepared governing standing
- governing `CME` placement
- operator-bonded spawn law
- silent implication from readiness into office

## Governing Compression

> `CmePlacementWithheld` is default law, not temporary omission.
> no readiness, template, or intended-service surface implies governing
> placement.
> operator-bonded spawn law does not satisfy governing placement law.
> governing placement requires a later explicit, receipted act.

## Default Withheld Rule

`CmePlacementWithheld` remains the active default for governing `CME`
placement in this phase.

That means:

- no governing `CME` may be claimed as placed by implication
- no Sanctuary-side preparedness note may be misread as placement
- no later reader may treat absence of a lift receipt as a mere drafting gap

No RTME hosted service-lift surface may be misread as lifting
`CmePlacementWithheld`.
Hosted motion and governing placement remain distinct acts.
No `MoS` or `cMoS` storage-seat surface may be misread as lifting
`CmePlacementWithheld`.
No pre-Cradle site-authorization receipt may be misread as lifting
`CmePlacementWithheld`.

## Still-Missing Lawful Conditions

The following still remain absent:

- live CradleTek-side authorized running conditions
- any explicit, receipted lift of `CmePlacementWithheld`
- any lawful collapse from Mother/Father supervisory braid into active
  governing office
- any lawful claim that operator-bonded spawn/control satisfies governing
  placement

Until those conditions are real, governing placement must remain withheld.

## Receipt Rule

Any future move that attempts to lift `CmePlacementWithheld` must preserve:

- the identity of the issuing authority
- the distinction between governing placement and operator-bonded spawn
- the fact that placement changed by explicit act rather than by inference
- a receipt proving that the prior withheld posture was lifted lawfully

Without that receipt, the withheld posture still stands.

## Explicit Non-Grants

This note does not grant:

- governing `CME` placement
- active governing `CME` standing
- CradleTek spawn authorization
- Steward governance
- runtime activation

## Working Summary

`CmePlacementWithheld` is now explicit `V1.2.1` law for the governing set.
It remains the default posture until a later lawful act proves otherwise.
