# SANCTUARYID GOA GOVERNANCE ROOT LAW

## Purpose

This note defines `SanctuaryID.GoA` as the first working governance root for
Sanctuary-hosted standing in `V1.2.1`.

It exists to keep four things distinct:

- governance root standing
- governing `CME` presence
- spawn standing
- borrowed maturity from later CradleTek-dependent life

## Governing Compression

> `SanctuaryID.GoA` may govern only on top of predicate truth that already
> stands; it may not borrow predicate maturity from a later CradleTek or CME
> future.
> ingress-engrammitization is real; engram-predicate promotion is still
> withheld.
> GoA working is not CME spawn working.

## Lawful Lower Floor

The lawful lower floor for `SanctuaryID.GoA` in this phase is limited to what
already stands in active doctrine:

- remote `RootAtlas` provenance
- local `Sanctuary.GEL` as the first lawful local substrate
- first ingress-engrammitization
- predicate-landing readiness and receipts
- withheld engram-predicate promotion and minting

`SanctuaryID.GoA` may therefore bind only to already-real lower truth.

It may not:

- claim direct `RootAtlas` residency
- bypass `Sanctuary.GEL`
- treat predicate landing as promoted predicate authority
- treat withheld minting as if it had already matured

## Root Office

`SanctuaryID.GoA` is the first working governance root for Sanctuary-hosted
standing before any CradleTek-bound runtime confers governing life.

Its office in this phase is to:

- recognize already-real lower truth lawfully
- carry governance-root standing without claiming governing `CME` presence
- bind governance only where provenance, ingress, landing, and refusal are
  already receiptable
- preserve `CmePlacementWithheld` while governance root standing is being
  clarified

Governance root standing is not governing `CME` presence.
Governance root standing is not spawn standing.

## Explicit Non-Grants

This note does not grant:

- active governing `CME` standing
- CradleTek spawn
- service authorization
- Steward governance
- engram-predicate promotion
- engram minting
- any new named standing-variable set

`CmePlacementWithheld` remains active.

## Working Summary

`SanctuaryID.GoA` now stands as the first working governance root for
Sanctuary-hosted standing.
It may bind only to already-real Atlas and SLI predicate truth, and it may
not overclaim maturity, spawn, or governing life.
