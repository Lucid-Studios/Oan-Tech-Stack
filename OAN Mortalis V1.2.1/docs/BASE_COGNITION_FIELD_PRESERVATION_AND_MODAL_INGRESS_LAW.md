# BASE_COGNITION_FIELD_PRESERVATION_AND_MODAL_INGRESS_LAW

## Purpose

This note mirrors the preserved-field law into the `V1.2.1` install-first
line.

Its job is to keep the sibling line from treating cognition as a flat store,
dominant ingress axis, or convenience retrieval surface while the first
runtime-bearing `SLI` families are still being carried forward.

## Governing Compression

Base cognition is a field that preserves form across ingress and egress.
Modal lanes deliver typed signals into pre-shaped reception geometry.
The envelope stabilizes those signals under invariant law.
No ingress may redefine the field.
No egress may distort its preserved form.

The strongest install-first read is:

> Base cognition is a field-preserving architecture rather than a flat store.
> Reception geometry exists before lawful ingress claims are made.
> No ingress lane may redefine the field.
> No egress surface may distort preserved form.

## Install-First Boundary

`V1.2.1` is not yet claiming multimodal runtime completion.

What is admitted now is:

- line-local preserved-field doctrine
- the first passive membrane corridor and observational organism in
  `San.Common`
- the first passive `SLI.Runtime` retrieval-facing family beneath that same
  membrane law

What remains withheld is:

- modality widening beyond the current symbolic analogue
- operator realization that acts across the preserved field
- any claim that field preservation already drives a full mature working line

## Current Symbolic Analogue

The present sibling line should still be read as the first symbolic analogue
of field preservation.

The current carried analogue is:

- modal ingress lane -> `RawSymbolicEnvelopeAdapter`
- prepared reception geometry -> `SymbolicEnvelope`
- invariant enforcement -> validator plus membrane decision policy
- field observation -> lane sink, witness snapshot, read model, inspection
- lawful retrieval review -> `FieldQuery`, `FieldQueryResult`,
  `QueryTensionSummary`
- lawful recomposition preparation -> `RecompositionCandidate`

This keeps the line honest about what exists:

> a field-preserving symbolic pipeline with first passive retrieval and
> recomposition discipline

## Non-Collapse Rules

1. No ingress lane may define the structure of the field.
2. No single modality may collapse the field into its own axis.
3. No egress surface may overwrite preserved invariants.
4. Reception geometry must exist before lawful ingress claims are made.
5. Query must not imply rewrite.
6. Recomposition must remain subordinate to preserved-field law.
