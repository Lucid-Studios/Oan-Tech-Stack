# SANCTUARYID GOA AUTHORITY AND RECEIPT LAW

## Purpose

This note defines what governance receipts may and may not mean at the
`SanctuaryID.GoA` governance root in `V1.2.1`.

It exists so legibility does not counterfeit maturity.

## Governing Compression

> governance receipts may witness what already stands, what has been bound,
> and what has been refused.
> they may not counterfeit promotion, minting, spawn, or governing life.

## Admissible Authority Surface

The admissible authority surface in this phase is limited to:

- provenance recognition
- `Sanctuary.GEL` substrate recognition
- ingress-engrammitization recognition
- predicate-landing recognition
- governance binding recognition
- refusal recognition

## Admissible Receipt Meanings

The only lawful receipt meanings in this phase are:

- provenance recognized
- `Sanctuary.GEL` substrate recognized
- ingress-engrammitization recognized
- predicate-landing recognized
- governance binding recognized
- refusal recognized

## Inadmissible Receipt Meanings

Receipts in this phase may not claim:

- engram-predicate promotion
- engram minting
- service authorization
- CradleTek spawn
- active governing `CME` existence
- Steward governance

## Explicit Non-Grants

This note does not authorize:

- runtime service standing
- governing `CME` presence
- CradleTek-dependent life
- any new named standing-variable set

## Working Summary

`SanctuaryID.GoA` receipts now have a bounded meaning.
They may witness provenance, substrate, ingress, landing, governance binding,
and refusal, while explicitly refusing to counterfeit promoted predicate
authority, spawn, or living governance.
