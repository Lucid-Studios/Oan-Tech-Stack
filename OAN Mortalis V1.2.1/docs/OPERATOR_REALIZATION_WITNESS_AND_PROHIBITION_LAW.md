# OPERATOR_REALIZATION_WITNESS_AND_PROHIBITION_LAW

## Purpose

This note fixes the witness requirements and non-negotiable prohibitions for
the later operator-realization seam.

It exists so future realization does not arrive through convenience shortcuts
or partial receipts.

## Governing Compression

No operator realization without witness.
No realization from unevaluated or disqualified candidates.
No seam passage may widen authority, persistence, or continuity.

The strongest working sentence is:

> A lawful candidate is not enough.
> The witnesses for lawful seam ingress must already exist.
> What is not witnessed may not cross.

## Required Witnesses Before Ingress Is Even Considered

Later operator realization may only be considered if all of the following are
already present:

- a bounded `RecompositionCandidate`
- a bounded `RecompositionCandidateEvaluationDecision`
- explicit source lineage and trace anchors
- explicit contradiction posture
- explicit membrane re-entry burden
- explicit candidate outcome and recovery class

## Seam Prohibitions

The following are hard red lines.

1. No operator realization from unevaluated recomposition candidates.
2. No operator realization from `defer` outcomes.
3. No operator realization from `reject` outcomes.
4. No authority widening during seam admission.
5. No persistence widening during seam admission.
6. No continuity widening during seam admission.
7. No `cleave` posture may be treated here as child issuance.
8. No missing witness may be inferred after ingress is requested.

## Witness Consequence

If required witness is absent, the correct result is not silent repair.

The correct result is:

- refusal of seam ingress
- retained candidate posture only
- explicit witness deficiency

## Relation To Candidate Evaluation

Candidate evaluation judges whether a candidate is fit to remain in lawful
preparation.

It does not:

- grant standing
- issue children
- realize operators
- bypass witness requirements

This note therefore sits immediately after candidate evaluation and
immediately before any future operator seam.
