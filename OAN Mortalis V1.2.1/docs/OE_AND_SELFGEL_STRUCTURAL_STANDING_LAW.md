# OE_AND_SELFGEL_STRUCTURAL_STANDING_LAW

## Purpose

This note defines the first structural occupants that may stand in
`Sanctuary.MoS`.

It exists so lawful storage-seat occupancy is separated from operator
realization, governing life, and automatic promotion from `GEL`.

## Governing Compression

> structural standing for `OE` and `SelfGEL` is not active governing life.
>
> no held `GEL` object promotes itself into `Sanctuary.MoS` occupancy.

## Structural-Standing Office

The first structural occupants admitted in this phase are:

- `OE`
- `SelfGEL`

Their bounded passive standing records now live in:

- `OeStructuralStandingRecord`
- `SelfGelStructuralStandingRecord`

## Structural Rule

Structural standing in this phase means:

- lawful seated memory occupancy
- lineage-bearing, receipted relation to `Sanctuary.MoS`
- no operator realization
- no active governing life
- no `CME` embodiment

Structural standing may not be inferred from:

- held `GEL` persistence alone
- `cGEL` build activity alone
- nearby presence of later governance language

## Occupant Boundary

`OE` and `SelfGEL` may stand structurally in `Sanctuary.MoS` without becoming:

- runtime bodies
- operator-realized offices
- governing `CME`s
- pre-Cradle authorization surfaces
