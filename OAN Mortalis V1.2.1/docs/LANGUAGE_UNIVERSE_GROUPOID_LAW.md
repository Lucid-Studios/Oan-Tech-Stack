# LANGUAGE_UNIVERSE_GROUPOID_LAW

## Purpose

This note defines how shared root ancestry may lawfully support multiple
universe-bound semantic formations without forcing identity.

It exists to keep `Sanctuary.GEL` from flattening common language,
discipline-local meaning, overlay analysis, and meta/control family lanes into
one undifferentiated surface.

## Governing Compression

> shared root ancestry does not imply shared semantic identity.
>
> no family tag, universe candidacy, or correspondence edge may be treated as
> semantic identity without explicit witness.

## Root Ancestry Versus Universe-Local Meaning

Atlas answers:

- what the ancestry root is

Language-universe groupoid answers:

- in which meaning-world that root may currently be operating

Shared root ancestry may support different predicate families and different
masters across different universes.

## Universe Classes

The admitted universe classes in this phase are:

- primary universe
- secondary universe
- overlay
- meta/control family

The first admitted primary universes are:

- `CommonLanguage`
- `Mathematics`
- `ScienceGeneral`
- `Sociology`

The first admitted overlays are:

- `Ontology`
- `Epistemology`

Meta/control family standing remains separate and non-equivalent to semantic
universes.

No secondary universe is admitted in this phase.

## Ingress Candidacy

Candidate plurality at ingress is lawful.

A token or span may carry more than one candidate universe without forced
collapse.

`family_projection_state` should record whether a family-tag projection is:

- `candidate`
- `provisional`
- `witnessed`

## Predicate Locality

Predicate formation is universe-local.

The same root ancestry may produce different predicate families in different
universes without requiring identity across them.

## Correspondence Edge Types

The admitted cross-universe correspondence edge types in this phase are:

- `equivalent`
- `analogous`
- `narrowed`
- `widened`
- `non-equivalent`
- `suspended`

## Canonical Fields

Universe-bearing semantic objects should carry at minimum:

- `root_ancestry_ref`
- `universe_candidates[]`
- `primary_universe`
- `overlay_refs[]`
- `predicate_family_ref`
- `correspondence_edges[]`
- `family_projection_state`

## Refusal Rules

The following must force refusal, hold, or non-identity:

- shared root with incompatible predicate structure advanced as identity
- overlay treated as a primary universe
- meta/control family treated as semantic universe
- cross-universe promotion without correspondence witness

## Explicit Non-Grants

This note does not grant:

- universe collapse by similarity alone
- overlay sovereignty
- meta/control semantic identity
- runtime meaning from family projection alone
- a GEL-native `TAG.sty` bridge

`TAG.sty` remains external ancestry only in this phase and is not yet admitted
as a GEL-native bridge surface.

## Working Summary

`Sanctuary.GEL` now fixes the first admitted universe ring and the first
non-collapsing law for shared root ancestry.

That law preserves candidate plurality, witnessed correspondence, and
universe-local predicate formation without forcing identity.
