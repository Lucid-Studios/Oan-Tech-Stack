# ROOTATLAS_MAPPING_AND_VARIANT_LAW

## Purpose

This note defines how preserved ingress maps into `RootAtlas` ancestry for
`Sanctuary.GEL` intake.

It exists to fix lawful root-family mapping without confusing remote source
ancestry with local formed knowledge.

## Governing Compression

> `RootAtlas` is root ancestry, not local formed knowledge.
>
> absence is evidence only when the absence is structurally expected and
> explicitly witnessed.

## Atlas Mapping Office

This office maps preserved ingress into:

- canonical roots
- root families
- variant relations
- inflection relations
- compound relations
- unresolved ambiguity

It is not allowed to:

- invent roots from fluency alone
- collapse variants without mapping
- treat remote ancestry as local knowledge continuity

The `ROOT_SYMBOL_BASE_FORM_LAW.md` note now governs shared root base-form
transport identity above this mapping office.

That means surface variation may remain mapped and witnessed here without
automatically becoming separate transport identity or contextual split.

## Required Mapping Fields

Every mapping candidate should carry at minimum:

- `atlas_root_id`
- `canonical_root`
- `raw_key`
- `raw_variants[]`
- `normalized_variants[]`
- `variant_relation`
- `language_origin`
- `symbol_class`
- `origin_universe`
- `source_witness`

## Variant Relations

The minimum variant relations in this phase are:

- `canonical`
- `inflection`
- `compound`
- `prefix-suffix-derived`
- `ambiguous`
- `unresolved`

`ambiguous` and `unresolved` may be retained.
They may not be silently flattened into `canonical`.

## Negative-Evidence Discipline

The intake path may learn from non-presence only when the absence is itself a
lawful signal.

That includes:

- expected family members not found
- required mapping links not found
- expected structural relation absent
- known compound pattern broken by omission

The intake path may not hallucinate meaning from mere lack.

## Refusal Law

The following must force refusal, hold, or non-promotion:

- root inferred without explicit token ancestry
- variant collapsed without relation record
- compound split without witness
- unresolved absence used as if it proved meaning
- remote source ancestry treated as local master knowledge

## Explicit Non-Grants

This note does not grant:

- proposition standing
- procedural standing
- runtime meaning
- direct local `RootAtlas` residency

## Working Summary

`Sanctuary.GEL` intake now has a lawful Atlas mapping office.

That office turns preserved ingress into witnessed root ancestry and variant
relation without pretending that roots are already formed knowledge or that
surface variation has already earned distinct transport identity.
