# CUSTODIAL AND BRAND CONTENT AUTHORITY LAW

## Purpose

This note defines the first role-valid authority split for content submitted
toward future `CME` customization or creation in `V1.2.1`.

It exists so custodial customization and brand/campaign preparation do not
collapse into one authority basis.

## Governing Compression

> custodial customization and brand/campaign preparation remain distinct
> authority bases.

## Role Separation

The following roles must remain distinct where they apply:

- operator
- rights holder
- subject of customization
- guardian/custodial authority
- corporate or brand principal

The bounded passive authority records now live in:

- `CustodialAuthorityRecord`
- `BrandAuthorityRecord`

## Authority Basis Rule

Custodial authority is the admitted basis when:

- a guardian acts for a child
- a guardian acts for a teen or young adult under lawful custodial footing

Brand authority is the admitted basis when:

- a brand principal
- campaign principal
- or corporate lead

prepares content for brand or campaign-bound use.

These authorities may coexist in one broader content-authority bundle, but
they may not substitute for one another silently.
