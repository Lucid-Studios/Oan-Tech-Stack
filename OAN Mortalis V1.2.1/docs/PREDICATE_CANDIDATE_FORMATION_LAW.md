# PREDICATE_CANDIDATE_FORMATION_LAW

## Purpose

This note defines the first relation-bearing semantic layer for
`Sanctuary.GEL` intake.

It exists to let roots become structured meaning candidates without claiming
closure, procedure, or runtime obligation.

## Governing Compression

> predicate candidate formation is the first structured meaning layer.
> it is still candidate-bearing, not closure-bearing.

## Predicate Candidate Office

This office forms relation-bearing candidate structures from lawful root
mapping.

It may include:

- identity relation
- role relation
- action relation
- modifier relation
- bounded graph relation

It is not allowed to:

- claim truth by fluency alone
- erase ambiguity by convenience
- imply procedure from relation alone

## Required Candidate Fields

Every predicate candidate should carry at minimum:

- `predicate_id`
- `root_refs[]`
- `relation_type`
- `arity`
- `binding_context`
- `universe_binding`
- `defined_terms_state`
- `context_scope_class`
- `evidence_sufficiency_state`

## Formation Conditions

A predicate candidate is formable only when:

- the referenced roots are classified
- variant ambiguity is either resolved or explicitly retained
- relation type is named
- universe binding is explicit
- the requested standing does not outrun candidate-only formation

## Malformation Discipline

Malformed or adversarial structure may be parsed into candidate evidence, but
it may not gain standing by being merely parseable.

Contradiction, stuffing, or structural overloading must remain explicit.

## Refusal Law

The following must force refusal, hold, or non-promotion:

- mixed-universe candidate structure without lawful mapping
- unstated relation type
- undefined terms advanced as if closure-bearing
- contradiction hidden inside candidate structure
- candidate structure promoted directly into proposition or procedure by
  convenience

## Explicit Non-Grants

This note does not grant:

- truth closure
- procedure standing
- runtime obligation
- active symbolic metabolism

## Working Summary

`Sanctuary.GEL` intake now has a lawful predicate-candidate office.

That office lets root ancestry become structured meaning while preserving the
bright line between candidate structure and closure-bearing standing.
