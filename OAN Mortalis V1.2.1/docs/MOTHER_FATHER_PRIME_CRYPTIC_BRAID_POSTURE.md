## Purpose

pre-body Sanctuary-side posture.

It exists to keep four things distinct:

- Mother/Father supervisory relation
- Prime/Cryptic duplex offices
- Steward governance
- admitted-body realization

## Governing Compression

> Mother/Father posture is not Steward governance.
> Prime recognizes and witnesses. Cryptic prepares and works.
> the supervisory braid is pre-body posture, not admitted-body realization.
> Sanctuary-side supervisory braid does not imply live CradleTek authority.

## Office Separation

`Mother/Father` and `Steward` are not interchangeable offices.

- constitutional world supervision
- pre-body oversight and witnessing
- local-world legality
- supervisory relation to what is still forming before admitted-body office

- admitted-body governance
- first protected office inside the installed CradleTek body
- a later office that must not be implied from Sanctuary-side preparation
