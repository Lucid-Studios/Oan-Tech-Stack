# CMOS_COE_AND_CSELFGEL_SURFACING_LAW

## Purpose

This note defines `Sanctuary.cMoS` and the derivational surfacing of `cOE`
and `cSelfGEL`.

It exists so cryptic derivation does not pretend to be a second sovereign
origin.

## Governing Compression

> `Sanctuary.cMoS` is the derivational private/cryptic companion to
> `Sanctuary.MoS`.
>
> `cOE` and `cSelfGEL` surface derivationally, not as a second sovereign
> origin.
>
> no direct `cGEL -> cOE/cSelfGEL` shortcut is lawful without the storage-seat
> corridor.

## `cMoS` Office

`Sanctuary.cMoS` is:

- derivational
- private/cryptic
- subordinate to one lawful parent `Sanctuary.MoS`
- the cryptic companion chamber from which derivative surfaces may later be
  read

The bounded passive companion record now lives in:

- `CMosSurfaceRecord`

## Derivational Surfacing Office

The only derivative surface kinds admitted in this phase are:

- `COe`
- `CSelfGel`

The bounded passive derivative records now live in:

- `CrypticDerivativeSurfaceKind`
- `CrypticDerivativeSurfacingRecord`

## Parent-Standing Rule

`cOE` and `cSelfGEL` may surface only when all of the following already exist:

- lawful parent `Sanctuary.MoS` seat standing
- lawful parent `Sanctuary.cMoS` derivational companionship
- lawful parent structural standing for `OE` or `SelfGEL`
- lawful receipted continuity from `GEL/cGEL` into `MoS/cMoS`

No direct `cGEL -> cOE/cSelfGEL` shortcut is admitted in this slice.

## Non-Sovereign Rule

`cOE` and `cSelfGEL` are:

- derivational
- subordinate
- continuity-bearing

They are not:

- a second sovereign origin
- active governing life
- governing `CME` placement
- pre-Cradle authorization

## Explicit Non-Grants

This note does not grant:

- cryptic self-origination of governance
- governing `CME` placement
- CradleTek authorization
- runtime governing loops

## Working Summary

`V1.2.1` now fixes `Sanctuary.cMoS` as the derivational private/cryptic
companion to `Sanctuary.MoS` and permits `cOE` and `cSelfGEL` to surface only
derivationally from lawful parent standing.
