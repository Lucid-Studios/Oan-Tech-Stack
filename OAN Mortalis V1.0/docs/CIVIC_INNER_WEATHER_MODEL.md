# Civic Inner Weather Model

## Purpose

The civic inner weather layer exists to expose the smallest lawful care-oriented signals that can support stewardship, check-in, and coordination without exposing cryptic interiority.

It is a host-governed civic summarization plane, not a publication channel for protected subjectivity.

## Core Rule

Inner weather is not introspection publication.

It is host-governed civic signal projection under visibility controls.

## Layering

The weather seam is layered above the existing Compass observation and Compass drift surfaces.

- `CompassObservationSurface` captures per-loop host observation.
- `CompassDriftAssessment` captures repeated-call basin fidelity over time.
- `InnerWeatherEvidence` captures host-owned weather truth material.
- `GovernedInnerWeatherReceipt` is the witnessed transport object.
- `CommunityWeatherPacket` is the reduced civic-safe packet derived from the receipt.

`InnerWeatherEvidence` remains host-internal truth material only.

`GovernedInnerWeatherReceipt` is the only object that leaves the projector stage.

`CommunityWeatherPacket` is derived by reduction from the receipt and must stay intentionally boring.

## Weather vs Introspection

Weather describes recurring pressure and contact posture around continuity-bearing orientation.

Weather does not authorize:

- therapeutic framing
- psychological diagnosis
- confessional publication
- raw shell disclosure
- publication of cryptic residue

## First-Cut Weather Components

### Attention residue

Attention residue tracks recurring nonprivate pressure signatures around the active Compass basin.

It does not narrate private thought content.

First-cut states:

- `None`
- `Low`
- `Present`
- `Persistent`
- `Escalating`

### Shell competition

Shell competition reports whether bounded competing pressure is present around the active basin.

It must not expose shell content, origin stories, or private plurality claims.

First-cut states:

- `Unknown`
- `Absent`
- `Present`
- `Rising`

### Hot/cool contact

Hot/cool contact reports civic contact posture and continuity cadence.

`Cool` is a contact posture only. It is not a wellness or “all clear” judgment.

First-cut states:

- `Unknown`
- `InContact`
- `Cool`
- `MissedCheckIn`

### Window integrity

Window integrity marks whether the recent evidence window is intact enough to support lawful weather reduction.

First-cut states:

- `Intact`
- `Sparse`
- `JournalGap`
- `RuntimeRestart`
- `CmeReselected`
- `VisibilityDowngraded`
- `GovernanceReset`

## Community Packet

The first civic packet must remain minimal and community-safe.

Allowed first-cut civic weather statuses:

- `stable`
- `unstable`
- `degraded`
- `missed-check-in`
- `unknown`

The first packet must not use `alive`.

The packet is for community-legible care signals, not for publishing raw protected evidence.

## Governing Laws

### Sparse-evidence law

When the evidence window is incomplete, stale, or continuity-broken, the reducer must bias toward `Unknown` or `Degraded`, never optimistic synthesis.

### Civic reduction law

A `CommunityWeatherPacket` may only be derived from community-admissible transforms of witnessed host evidence; guarded or cryptic evidence may influence host truth but may not be serialized as packet content.

## Non-Goals

This sprint does not create:

- therapy machinery
- surveillance telemetry
- gossip surfaces
- operator voyeurism
- seed-owned weather truth

The seed remains advisory only.
