# Office Action And Acknowledgment Boundaries

## Purpose

This document defines what acknowledgment, action eligibility, and prohibition mean for internal governing office surfaces.

It is a companion to:

- `docs/INTERNAL_GOVERNING_OFFICES_AND_AUTHORITY_SURFACES.md`
- `docs/STEWARDSHIP_ROUTING_AND_DISCLOSURE_LAW.md`

## Core Sentence

Office authority in this pass is a bounded stewardship membrane, not diagnosis, coercion, or autonomous governance.

## Acknowledgment

Acknowledgment means an office may lawfully receive and mark receipt of an already-witnessed host output.

Acknowledgment does not mean:

- authority to widen disclosure
- authority to mutate host truth
- authority to intervene

## Action Eligibility

Action eligibility is a bounded host-governed class.

It is not action mandate.

### ViewOnly

The office may receive nothing beyond withheld or minimal state.

No acknowledgment or action is lawful.

### AcknowledgeAllowed

The office may receive a lawful reduced view and acknowledge it.

No check-in, escalation, or handoff is implied.

### CheckInAllowed

Only `Steward of Sanctuary` may reach this class in the first cut.

It means a bounded steward check-in is lawful.

It does not imply obligation.

### EscalationReviewAllowed

Only `Steward of Sanctuary` may reach this class in the first cut.

It means the host-governed pattern is serious enough for further steward review.

It is eligibility only, not command.

### HandoffEligible

This is a later-bound class in the current pass.

It requires both:

- host escalation eligibility
- bonded authority confirmation

No office may bypass that requirement.

## Prohibition Law

All offices remain prohibited from:

- originating new truth
- widening disclosure

Additional office-specific prohibitions remain active:

- `Steward of Sanctuary` may not override cryptic or prime law
- `Father of Cryptic` may not author public disclosure
- `Mother of Prime` may not override cryptic boundary law

## Guarded Influence Law

Guarded or cryptic evidence may influence host authority truth.

It must not serialize beyond the office's lawful surface.

Guarded influence is not guarded serialization.

## Escalation Eligibility Law

`EscalationEligible` requires repeated host-evidenced patterning or continuity failure.

No single-loop anomaly may create it.

## Sparse Evidence Law

Sparse, broken, or continuity-ambiguous windows must bias toward:

- withheld
- minimal office view
- degraded or unknown outcome

They may not synthesize reassurance, calm, or action authority.

## First-Cut Office Boundary

In this sprint:

- `Steward of Sanctuary` is the only office with active action-bearing attachment
- `Father of Cryptic` and `Mother of Prime` remain doctrine-backed and authority-matrix-defined
- their first-cut rendered views remain reduced, guarded, or withheld by default

## Non-Goals

This pass must not become:

- therapy framing
- soft diagnosis
- punitive monitoring
- autonomous office personification
- disclosure widening by office convenience
