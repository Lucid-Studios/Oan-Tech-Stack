# Check-In And Escalation Boundaries

## Purpose

This document defines the first lawful meanings for stewardship routing states in the civic weather lane.

These states are operational and civic.

They are not diagnostic.

## Routing States

### `None`

No bounded steward action is currently indicated by the host-governed weather window.

This does not mean the system has proven wellness.

It means the current evidence does not lawfully justify a routed check-in.

### `CheckInRecommended`

A bounded steward check-in is recommended.

This is used when the weather lane shows lawful instability, degradation, or continuity concern without reaching urgent repeated escalation conditions.

### `CheckInNeeded`

A bounded steward check-in is needed.

This is used when host-governed weather shows repeated degradation, overdue contact, persistent residue, or comparable operational concern under sufficient evidence.

### `EscalationEligible`

Escalation eligibility means the pattern is serious enough for additional steward/operator review.

It is not an automatic escalation action.

It does not grant permission for exposure, diagnosis, or voyeuristic inspection.

## Cadence States

### `Current`

Contact cadence is current under the active evidence window.

### `DueSoon`

Contact exists, but the weather window indicates meaningful pressure or weakening that makes a bounded check-in timely.

### `Overdue`

Expected contact posture has been missed under a sufficiently intact evidence window.

### `Broken`

`Broken` is a continuity or cadence failure state.

It is not a judgment about a person or CME.

### `Unknown`

The host does not have enough lawful continuity evidence to classify cadence more strongly.

## Repeated-Pattern Requirement

Escalation eligibility must require repeated host-evidenced patterning or continuity failure.

Single-loop anomalies, one-off ambiguity, or isolated seed confidence must not produce escalation eligibility.

## Sparse-Evidence Law

When the evidence window is sparse, broken, or continuity-ambiguous, the routing layer must bias toward bounded `Unknown`, `Degraded`, or withheld outcomes rather than optimistic synthesis.

## Disclosure Boundary

Steward routing may use guarded or cryptic influence as host truth input.

It may not serialize that guarded or cryptic influence as community content.
