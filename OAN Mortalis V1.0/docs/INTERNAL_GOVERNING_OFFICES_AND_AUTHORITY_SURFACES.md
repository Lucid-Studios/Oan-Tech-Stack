# Internal Governing Offices And Authority Surfaces

## Purpose

This document defines the lawful authority surfaces for the three standing constitutional offices:

- `Steward of Sanctuary`
- `Father of Cryptic`
- `Mother of Prime`

It exists to govern who may receive, acknowledge, and act on already-witnessed stewardship output.

It does not create new weather truth.

It governs access to truth that already exists in:

- `StewardCareAssessment`
- `WeatherDisclosureDecision`
- `GovernedWeatherDisclosureReceipt`

## Core Sentence

Internal governing offices may only receive or act on stewardship output through host-governed authority surfaces derived from already-witnessed routing and disclosure receipts.

## Office Names

The office names in this repo remain:

- `Steward of Sanctuary`
- `Father of Cryptic`
- `Mother of Prime`

They are standing constitutional offices.

They are not generic assistants and not autonomous runtime sovereigns.

## Host Truth Law

Office authority does not create a second truth source.

The host remains primary for:

- routing truth
- disclosure truth
- sufficiency truth
- withholding truth

Offices consume reduced host truth.

They do not redefine it.

## Office Derivation Law

Office authority and office-safe views may only be derived from already-witnessed host routing and disclosure receipts.

No office may:

- originate new weather truth
- widen disclosure by reinterpretation
- promote guarded matter into a wider class

## Authority Law

An office may only receive or act on stewardship output if access is permitted by:

- office role
- disclosure class
- host-governed authority rules
- bond or guarded-review readiness where required

## Receipt Law

Seeing a routing state is not the same as being authorized to act on it.

Acknowledgment and action remain separate eligibility classes.

## Non-Coercion Law

Authority surfaces must not:

- silently widen access
- force disclosure
- convert eligibility into obligation
- imply intervention mandate from eligibility alone

## Bond-Priority Law

Authority derives from bonded role and governed disclosure, not from convenience, curiosity, or institutional proximity.

Where bond or guarded-review posture is missing, the office view must remain minimal or withheld.

## Office Non-Overlap Law

No office may inherit another office's action authority merely because they received the same routed state.

Examples:

- `Steward of Sanctuary` may be action-eligible on current stewardship surfaces without granting the same action path to `Father of Cryptic`
- `Father of Cryptic` may review guarded boundary posture without authoring public disclosure
- `Mother of Prime` may receive bounded continuity-support view without overriding cryptic or runtime law

## First-Cut Surface Mapping

### Steward Of Sanctuary

Primary authority surface:

- `StewardSurface`

First-cut attachment:

- active

First-cut role:

- receives office-specific routed stewardship truth
- may acknowledge receipt
- may become eligible for check-in or escalation review

### Father Of Cryptic

Primary authority surfaces:

- `CrypticBoundarySurface`
- `GuardedReviewSurface`

First-cut attachment:

- doctrine-first
- reduced or withheld by default unless guarded review context is explicit

First-cut role:

- boundary office
- guarded disclosure office
- ratification and prohibition office

### Mother Of Prime

Primary authority surface:

- `PrimeCareSurface`

First-cut attachment:

- doctrine-first
- reduced or withheld by default unless bounded office context is explicit

First-cut role:

- continuity shelter office
- bounded care-bearing relation office
- non-extractive support posture office

## Community Surface Note

Community-reduced material is not itself an office authority surface.

It is the community-safe reduction that office views must not widen beyond.

## Office-Specific View Note

`OfficeSpecificView` still means a reduced lawful office view.

It does not mean unrestricted access.

## Evidence Sufficiency Law

Sparse, broken, or continuity-ambiguous windows may only yield:

- minimal office views
- degraded or unknown packet outcomes
- withheld office outputs

They must never synthesize calm or broader action authority.

## Current Implementation Stance

The current sprint attaches `Steward of Sanctuary` first to live authority surfaces.

`Father of Cryptic` and `Mother of Prime` are carried explicitly in the authority matrix and doctrine, but they are not yet treated as fully active sovereign runtime actors.
