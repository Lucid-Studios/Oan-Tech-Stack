# Theater-Scoped Uptake Conformance Model

## Purpose

This note defines the lawful uptake layer between tensor resonance and SLI/Lisp actualization.

OAN is deliberately developing an IUTT-derived conformance framework for engineered cognition. Repo terms remain primary. No literal one-to-one formal identity with Mochizuki's formal objects is claimed unless exact correspondences are explicitly specified.

This pass keeps `GEL` as the repo-native storage and library term. It does not introduce a parallel `GLE` term.

## Governing Lines

> The tensor pass may be resonant, but the SLI/Lisp pass must be lawful.
>
> SLI/Lisp may shape what is uptake-worthy. It must not decide what is accountable.
>
> Theater-scoped uptake is not witness, not transport, not admissibility, and not Sanctuary judgment.

## Upstream Bridge

The lawful upstream bridge is:

1. `English/query surface`
2. `tensor resonance / candidate neighborhoods`
3. `root anchoring`
4. `theater selection`
5. `engram kind typing`
6. `observer-position declaration`
7. `relative-vs-absolute carrier decision`
8. `local atlas / functor plan`
9. `invariant-core extraction`
10. `obstruction / consistency qualification`
11. `SLI/Lisp actualization`
12. later `witness -> transport -> admissible surface -> accountability packet -> Sanctuary judgment`

This means the uptake seam is pre-witness and pre-transport by definition.

It also means resonance may propose neighborhoods, but only lawful conformance may actualize an uptake object.

## Conformance Disciplines

OAN-native terms remain primary in this note. IUTT-derived language appears only as secondary mapping.

### 1. Engram Kind Discipline (`species`)

Every uptake candidate must declare what kind of object it is before SLI/Lisp may actualize it.

This prevents the stack from treating a matched token cloud as though it were already a lawful engram object.

### 2. Observer-Position Discipline (`basepoint`)

Every uptake candidate must declare the stance from which it is being formed.

This prevents silent leakage of subjectivity into allegedly viewless symbolic objects.

### 3. Relative Carrier Discipline (`torsor`)

Some candidate structures must remain relative carriers rather than silently absolute coordinates.

This applies especially to:

- self/other distinction
- perspective
- role
- participatory relation

### 4. Theater-Local Assembly Discipline

Meaning is assembled locally inside a lawful Theater and may not be globally flattened for convenience.

Theater selection therefore happens before any later witness, transport, admissibility, or accountability claim.

### 5. Invariant-Core Discipline (`mutation-core`)

What survives candidate formation is not the whole neighborhood, but the invariant-bearing core that remains lawful under later change.

This is the first bounded answer to the question, "what still remains after hammering?"

### 6. Obstruction Discipline

An uptake candidate may fail actualization lawfully.

`ConsistencyState` and `CohomologyState` are the right doctrinal places for that refusal.

An object may therefore be:

- locally suggestive
- resonantly interesting
- globally obstructed

without the stack treating that failure as an error in need of concealment.

### 7. Provenance Discipline

Every uptake candidate must carry provenance strongly enough that later surfaces can distinguish:

- where it came from
- what was inferred
- what was only resonantly proposed
- what remains deferred

This includes self-implicating statements and reported world events. A sentence such as "I saw you driving on Monday" remains a provenance-bearing claim until lawful validation confirms what part, if any, belongs to stabilized self-memory, schedule truth, or capability truth.

## Doctrinal Boundaries

The uptake seam must preserve these boundaries:

- resonance may propose neighborhoods, but resonance alone does not actualize a lawful object
- self, other, perspective, and role may be relative carriers rather than silently absolute coordinates
- consistency and cohomology may block actualization; failure is lawful
- uptake may form candidate packets, but it may not collapse into identity, custody, `GEL` append, or Sanctuary review
- contradiction between hot runtime uptake and cooler validation surfaces must produce validation routing or obstruction, not invented reconciliation
- a tensor neighborhood is not yet an `EngramObject`
- an `UptakePacket` is not yet a witness
- an `UptakePacket` is not yet a transport permission
- an `UptakePacket` is not yet an admissible surface
- an `UptakePacket` is not yet a Sanctuary judgment
