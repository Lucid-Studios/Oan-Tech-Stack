# BOUNDED_DREAM_GAME_REHEARSAL_MODEL

## Purpose

This document defines branch-local exploratory cognition after perspectival and participatory configuration, but before admissibility and before shared-truth claims.

For this doctrine pass, `Dream` and `Game` remain paired as one bounded exploratory lane unless later doctrine separates them more sharply.

This document is doctrine only. It does not add runtime code, contracts, schemas, services, interfaces, or wiring.

## 1. Bounded Dream/Game Rehearsal

Bounded Dream/Game rehearsal is the branch-local exploratory lane in which already situated and participatory cognition may lawfully rehearse alternate constructions without claiming truth, identity, or admissibility.

At this threshold:

- branching is permitted
- substitution is permitted under law
- analogy is permitted under law
- candidate local worlds may be explored

At this threshold it is still true that:

- rehearsal outputs are pre-admissible
- rehearsal outputs are non-binding
- rehearsal outputs are not Sanctuary intake

## 2. Lawful Substitution

Lawful substitution means a local element may be replaced for exploratory purposes only when the branch remains explicitly bounded and the replacement does not counterfeit identity, truth, or accountability.

Lawful substitution may involve:

- alternate role placement
- alternate contextual loading
- alternate predicate framing
- alternate local continuity assumptions

Lawful substitution must not:

- silently rewrite canonical identity
- silently rewrite Prime or Cryptic commitment
- silently promote branch matter into admissible state

## 3. Lawful Analogy

Lawful analogy means the system may compare or map structures across branches without claiming that analogous structures are already the same structure.

Analogy may support:

- exploratory comparison
- branch-local rehearsal
- candidate-form generation
- provisional pattern transfer

Analogy must not be treated as:

- proof of identity
- proof of transport
- proof of admissibility

## 4. Branch-Local Exploratory Candidate Formation

Dream/Game rehearsal may produce exploratory candidate forms such as:

- alternate local-world constructions
- role inversions
- branch-local relation patterns
- candidate continuities that still require later testing

These are exploratory outputs only.

They are not yet:

- lawful witnesses
- transport-ready correspondences
- admissible surfaces

## 5. Identity Seals And Non-Binding Boundaries

The exploratory lane must remain sealed away from automatic identity mutation.

Minimum boundaries:

- no automatic continuity binding
- no automatic Prime fact status
- no automatic Cryptic guarantee status
- no automatic Sanctuary accountability

These seals make rehearsal safe enough to be useful without letting it counterfeit stronger stages.

## 6. Why Dream/Game Remains Pre-Admissible

Dream/Game rehearsal remains pre-admissible because exploratory productivity is not the same thing as governable observability.

A rehearsal output may be structurally interesting, symbolically fertile, or locally coherent while still failing the thresholds for:

- review
- governance intake
- lawful transport
- identity-bearing admissibility

## 7. Relationship To Later Thresholds

Bounded Dream/Game rehearsal prepares conditions for later morphism candidacy, but it does not define witness-bearing correspondence by itself.

Later doctrine must still determine:

- preserved invariants
- lawful difference
- residue
- gluing thresholds
- the transition from candidacy into lawful transport
