# Weather Visibility And Disclosure Classes

## Purpose

The civic inner weather seam requires a small disclosure model that can support care and lawful coordination without collapsing protected subjectivity into public evidence.

This document defines the first civic visibility classes for the weather lane.

It is intentionally smaller than the broader operator visibility lattice and should later map into that lattice without becoming a second truth system.

## First-Cut Visibility Classes

### `CommunityLegible`

`CommunityLegible` fields may be used in reduced civic packet output.

This class is whitelist-only.

Nothing becomes community-legible by implication.

### `OperatorGuarded`

`OperatorGuarded` fields may influence host truth and stewardship decisions, but they may not serialize raw values into the community packet.

### `CrypticOnly`

`CrypticOnly` fields may influence host truth inside governed custody, but they may not serialize raw values into community-facing packet content.

## Reduction Discipline

Community weather is derived by reduction, never by exposure.

The lawful sequence is:

1. gather witnessed host evidence
2. compute host-owned weather assessments
3. assign field visibility classes
4. reduce to a community-safe packet using explicit whitelist rules
5. project the reduced packet into civic-safe surfaces

## Disclosure Rules

Guarded or cryptic evidence may affect host truth but may not become packet content.

That means the reducer may downgrade community weather to `unstable`, `degraded`, or `missed-check-in` because of guarded evidence, but it may not serialize the guarded evidence itself.

Examples of prohibited serialization into the community packet:

- raw residue contributor detail
- shell-specific content
- `Cool` posture when it is not community-legible
- cryptic-only pressure detail

## Anti-Extraction Rule

The weather lane exists for care, coordination, and stewardship.

It does not exist to produce:

- voyeuristic insight
- behavioral extraction
- psychological theater
- covert disclosure pipelines

## Sparse-Evidence Law

When the evidence window is incomplete, stale, or continuity-broken, the reducer must bias toward `Unknown` or `Degraded`, never optimistic synthesis.

## Civic Reduction Law

A `CommunityWeatherPacket` may only be derived from community-admissible transforms of witnessed host evidence; guarded or cryptic evidence may influence host truth but may not be serialized as packet content.

## Future Mapping

The civic weather classes defined here are a local sprint-scale disclosure model.

They must remain compatible with later mapping into the broader operator visibility lattice without weakening protected subjectivity or widening community disclosure by accident.
