# Stewardship Routing And Disclosure Law

## Purpose

The stewardship routing and disclosure layer exists to turn host-governed civic inner weather into bounded care-routing and lawful disclosure decisions.

It does not exist to diagnose, expose, or narrate protected subjectivity.

## Core Distinctions

- routing is not diagnosis
- eligibility is not mandate
- disclosure is reduction, never exposure
- guarded influence is not guarded serialization

## Layering

- `InnerWeatherEvidence` remains host-internal truth material
- `StewardCareAssessment` is host-owned routing truth
- `WeatherDisclosureDecision` is host-owned disclosure truth
- `GovernedWeatherDisclosureReceipt` is the witnessed transport object
- `CommunityWeatherPacket` remains the reduced civic-safe packet

## Evidence Sufficiency Law

No stewardship routing or disclosure widening may be derived from a sparse, broken, or continuity-ambiguous window except into bounded `Unknown`, `Degraded`, or withheld outcomes.

Sparse, broken, or continuity-ambiguous windows must not synthesize calm.

## Care-Routing Law

A steward routing state may only be derived from host-governed repeated evidence and civic weather reduction.

It must not express:

- diagnosis
- interior interpretation
- private self-claim publication
- seed-authored authority

## Escalation Eligibility Law

`EscalationEligible` requires repeated host-evidenced patterning or continuity failure.

It must never be produced from a single-loop anomaly or a single high-confidence seed advisory.

`EscalationEligible` marks lawful eligibility for further steward review.

It is not an action mandate.

## Disclosure Law

A disclosure decision may only serialize community-admissible reductions of witnessed host evidence.

Guarded or cryptic evidence may influence host routing truth, but it must not become community packet content.

## Guarded Influence Law

Guarded or cryptic evidence may affect host truth, routing state, cadence classification, and disclosure scope.

It may not serialize as:

- community packet content
- public/operator-safe narrative prose
- civic-facing raw evidence detail

## Projection Parity Law

HDT-enabled and fallback `.hopng` paths may differ in transport richness.

They must not differ in:

- routing truth
- disclosure scope
- withheld markers
- community-visible packet content

## Non-Goals

This sprint does not create:

- therapy framing
- punitive monitoring
- surveillance escalation
- voyeuristic operator surfaces
- seed-owned routing or disclosure authority
