# Prime Citizen Science Access Model

This note describes the public access posture for OAN technology. It is a
descriptive public model, not a build manual, product release, certification
program, legal conclusion, or operational authorization.

## Purpose

The public-facing access model treats the Industrial Base as a citizen science
education and stewardship layer. Public participation should begin with shared
language, safety expectations, non-claims, and review boundaries before any
future approved operational use.

## Public Access Layer

Public materials may support:

- education about purpose, safety, and accountability;
- stewardship literacy for reviewers, contributors, and partners;
- civic participation around responsible technology use;
- non-executable examples of public wording and review gates;
- future module orientation at a high level, when it remains descriptive.

Public materials should not support:

- build reconstruction;
- private corpus interpretation;
- deployment, release, or runtime operation;
- source extraction;
- dataset, fixture, or payload publication;
- authority, custody, identity, or legal claims.

## Certification And Approval Boundary

Citizen uptake requires more than public reading access. Future operational use
should depend on education, certification, approval, and stewardship review
appropriate to the role, context, and risk.

Public documentation can describe that boundary. It should not grant access,
issue authority, or replace private review.

## Private Engineering Boundary

Private engineering materials remain withheld unless a separate release review
approves a reduced public excerpt. Withheld materials include source-bearing
work, internal architecture, private research bodies, machine-local assumptions,
runtime payloads, and operational recipes.

## Stewardship Rule

When public explanation and private capability conflict, the public repository
should prefer clarity without reconstruction. The goal is informed public
understanding, not uncontrolled technical replication.

## Future Public Work

Future public surfaces may expand into education, formal publications,
newsletter-style summaries, videos, or module-specific overviews. Each expansion
should remain descriptive until reviewed for rights, privacy, security,
dual-use risk, operational maturity, and claim accuracy.
