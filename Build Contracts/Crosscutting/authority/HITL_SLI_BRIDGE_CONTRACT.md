# HITL SLI Bridge Contract

## Purpose

This contract fixes the active build reading for the bridge between human
lawful participation and continuity-bearing interior structure.

The primary bridge is not:

- `English -> Lisp`

The primary bridge is:

- `HITL -> SLI`

English and Lisp may remain important media in practice, but they are not the
first architectural distinction.

## Core Read

`HITL` is the witness-bearing ingress surface.

It carries:

- shared-prime-reality contact
- witness and contextual judgment
- consent and role-bound participation
- trust invariants and correction pressure
- bounded reality contact before inward carry-forward

`SLI` is the continuity-bearing interior surface.

It carries:

- lawful continuity
- compositional transformation
- memory-bearing form
- recursive structured operation
- admissible carry-forward

The bridge exists so that witnessed human participation can become structured
interior continuity without distortion or premature collapse.

## No-Collapse Rule

The bridge must not be flattened into:

- prompt ingress on one side
- symbolic execution on the other side

That would lose the actual law of the system.

The stronger rule is:

- `HITL` must not be reduced to prompt residue
- `SLI` must not drift away from witnessed reality
- the bridge must remain alive long enough for witness to become lawful
  continuity

## Invariants

The active bridge invariants are:

1. no silent widening
   nothing admitted on the interior side may outrun witnessed ingress,
   authorization, scope, or burden
2. no loss of obligation
   trust, contract, and role-nexus constraints must survive transit
3. no counterfeit certainty
   uncertainty or incompleteness on the interior side must return as explicit
   uncertainty rather than smoothed confidence
4. round-trip integrity
   outward return must preserve meaningful structure rather than only surface
   phrasing
5. downgrade propagation
   breach or mismatch in the interior side must trigger lawful clarify,
   escalate, review, or re-anchoring behavior on the outward side

## Build Read

For the active `V1.1.1` line, this bridge should currently be read as:

- `HITL` ingress through Sanctuary-owned witness and constitutional beginning
- `SLI` continuation through Prime/Cryptic transit, hosted symbolic medium, and
  continuity-bearing carry-forward
- guarded listening space between them, where lawful emergence may still remain
  intentionally unresolved

This means an undefined or held seam is not automatically a defect.

When the bridge is working lawfully, some spaces remain open until witness,
readiness, or formation makes a stronger structure admissible.

## Media Clarification

The current practical media read remains:

- English often carries `HITL` witness, law, and relational ingress
- Lisp or a Lisp-like symbolic medium may carry interior `SLI` composition and
  transformation

But the governing distinction is still:

- `HITL` outside as witness-bearing ingress
- `SLI` inside as continuity-bearing form

So implementation media must not replace the deeper bridge law.

This contract directly supports the current build-hold posture:

- chapter five as truth-carriage hinge
- Steward uptake before later developmental widening
- early `SoulFrame` bootstrap as lawful plumbing rather than premature interior

## Working Summary

The current law is:

- `HITL` witnesses reality into the bond
- `SLI` carries that witness forward as lawful continuity
- transit must preserve scope, burden, uncertainty, and downgrade law
- implementation media are subordinate to that bridge, not the other way around
