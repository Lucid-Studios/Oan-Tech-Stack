# POST PRIME CLOSURE CONTINUITY LAW

## Purpose

This note defines the first bounded post-closure field continuity seam for the
active `V1.1.1` line.

It exists to keep five things distinct:

- explicit Prime closure attestation
- the remaining product that stands
- the field that still bears lawful continuity
- deferred or bounded edges that remain visible
- post-closure re-entry that may remain possible without falsifying prior
  passage

Post-closure continuity shall attest what remains lawfully active after explicit Prime closure, including active carriers, lawful residues, deferred edges, and bounded re-entry paths.
closure of a product shall not imply voiding of the bearing field, and continuity shall not imply that all field matter remains equally open or unresolved.

## Core Read

The active stack should now read post-closure continuity this way:

- Prime closure says whether a lawful product now stands
- post-closure continuity says what remains lawfully active around that standing
- active carriers may remain in circulation without reopening closure by
  implication
- lawful residues may remain visible without being treated as unresolved
  failure
- deferred edges may remain explicitly open at the closure boundary when the
  field is not lawfully empty
- bounded re-entry may remain possible without pretending prior passage was
  void

In compact form:

> Post-closure continuity shall attest what remains lawfully active after
> explicit Prime closure, including active carriers, lawful residues, deferred
> edges, and bounded re-entry paths; closure of a product shall not imply
> voiding of the bearing field, nor shall continuity imply that all field
> matter remains equally open or unresolved.

## First Continuity Kinds

The first bounded post-closure continuity family is:

- `FieldContinuing`
- `CarrierActive`
- `ResidueLawful`
- `DeferredEdgeOpen`
- `ReentryPermitted`
- `ContinuityWithheld`

These are not interchangeable.
They are the smallest family by which the stack may say how the field remains
alive, bounded, and non-void after the closure boundary has been read.

## Non-Void Rule

The first post-closure continuity rule is:

- the closed product and the continuing field must remain distinguishable
- active carriers may continue without re-opening closure by implication
- lawful residues may remain visible without being flattened into unresolved
  burden
- deferred edges may remain visible without being falsely called completion
- bounded re-entry may remain lawful only when prior passage, preserved
  distinction, adjacent possibility, and non-consumptive relation to `GEL`
  remain attested

That means continuity is not a fake reset and not a claim that everything
continues equally.
It is an attestation that the field is still non-void in a bounded and
truthful way.

## First Invariant

The first post-closure continuity invariant is:

> Post-closure continuity may attest what remains active around a closed
> product, but it may not erase preserved distinction, void the bearing field,
> consume GEL substrate, or pretend that all field matter remains equally open
> once one form has lawfully stabilized.

That means:

- `FieldContinuing` is lawful when the field remains non-void in bounded form
- `CarrierActive` is lawful when symbolic carriers remain in circulation
- `ResidueLawful` is lawful when lawful residues remain visible around the
  standing product
- `DeferredEdgeOpen` is lawful when the closure boundary still preserves
  deferred edges visibly
- `ReentryPermitted` is lawful only when bounded re-entry remains explicitly
  attested
- `ContinuityWithheld` is lawful whenever continuity would otherwise be faked

## Immediate Build Consequence

Any future post-closure continuity seam in this area should say plainly:

- what Prime closure record it consumed
- what reopening-bearing record it consumed
- what continuity kind was lawful
- what active carriers remained visible
- what lawful residues remained visible
- what deferred edges remained visible
- what re-entry conditions remained attested
- that the bearing field remained non-void without pretending everything
  continued equally

If it cannot say that plainly, the stack should keep post-closure continuity
withheld rather than pretending that closure made the field either empty or
equally alive in every direction.
