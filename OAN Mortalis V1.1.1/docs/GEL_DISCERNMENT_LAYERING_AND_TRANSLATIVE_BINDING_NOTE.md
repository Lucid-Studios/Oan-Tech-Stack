`OAN Mortalis V1.1.1`.

## Layered GEL

`GEL` is the first retained field of governed meaning in the active line.

It is not a uniform container where every retained object is treated as the
same kind of thing with a different payload. The line keeps a small shared
constitutional envelope while preserving the lawful difference between layers.

The active discernment layers are:

- `Root`
  - irreducible meaning unit, not yet definition or explanation
  - held without expansion
  - eligible for later stabilization into definition
- `Dictionary`
  - stabilized bounded definition grounded in `Root`
  - admitted only after meaning has boundary and root-grounding
  - not a license for encyclopedic or procedural spill

The named-but-withheld layers are:

- `Encyclopedic`
  - reserved for lawful contextual or relational expansion
  - not operational in this slice
- `Procedural`
  - reserved for action-bearing or instruction-bearing structure
  - not operational in this slice

## Layer Integrity

The line now treats discernment as a property of `GEL` itself, not only of
pre-admission screening and not only of later `SLI` translation.

That means the active constitutional reading is:

- ingress discernment asks what may enter
- retained discernment asks what may remain
- translative discernment asks what may lawfully move without collapse

This note names the layering seam. It does not yet activate executable
residency governance over retained branches.

## Translative Binding

`SLI` does not replace `GEL`, and it does not create a fifth layer.

`SLI` is the translative binding surface that:

- preserves layer-kind during transport
- preserves anchor lineage during transport
- refuses unauthorized promotion across layers
- exposes carriers that can later support engram candidacy

In the active slice:

- `Root` binds through the placeholder carrier `sli.root.carrier`
- `Dictionary` binds through the placeholder carrier `sli.definition.carrier`

These are carrier placeholders, not live executable symbolic grammars.
