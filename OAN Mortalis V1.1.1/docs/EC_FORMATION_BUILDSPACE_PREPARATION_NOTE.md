# EC_FORMATION_BUILDSPACE_PREPARATION_NOTE

## Purpose

This note prepares the first bounded build space for the `EC`-facing formation
seam in `V1.1.1`.

It defines the smallest code-bearing preparation layer that can track:

- orientation
- cone boundary
- source relation
- requirement state
- why-not classification

This is a build-space preparation note.
It is not full `EC` runtime activation, not live `ListeningFrame` behavior, and
not dimensional cognition canonization.

## First Law

The first law for this preparation seam is:

> Cognitive formation within Sanctuary shall classify orientation, cone
> boundary, need, and incompletion explicitly, and shall produce only the
> smallest next truthful act supported by the current cone of evidence.

The first extension law for failure under pressure is:

> A cognitive traversal is only lawful if self remains the locus of
> orientation, failure remains legible as field disclosure, and no collapse
> signature is falsely promoted into stable knowledge.

## Minimal Behavioral Floor

The initial code seam must preserve these distinctions:

- `Present`
- `Missing`
- `Blocked`
- `Unknown`
- `NotRequired`

It must also preserve these constraints:

- nothing outside the cone may be promoted to known
- `Missing` may not collapse into `Blocked`
- `Blocked` may not collapse into `Unknown`
- `NotRequired` may not masquerade as `Satisfied`
- failure may only become orientation evidence when zed-locus preservation and
  minimum orientation integrity are both explicitly attested
- the evaluator prefers the smallest truthful next act over speculative uplift
- deferred and refused outcomes still emit a retained receipt

## Relation To Existing Owners

This preparation seam does not replace the current executable-facing owners.

It remains subordinate to:

- `ListeningFrame`
  - the posture field and place-from-which owner
- `Compass`
  - the orientation working set and admissibility read
- `Engineered Cognition`
  - the broader bounded cognition body

The preparation seam exists so those later owners can land on explicit
formation bookkeeping instead of implied cognition.

receipt in `EngineeredCognitionFirstPrimeStateContracts.cs`, so `EC` may be
walked from admitted install and cast `AgentiCore` sensor body through
stable-one discernment into Prime retained standing without applying
