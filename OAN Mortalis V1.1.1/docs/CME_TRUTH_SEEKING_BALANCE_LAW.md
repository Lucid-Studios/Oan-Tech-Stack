# CME TRUTH SEEKING BALANCE LAW

## Purpose

This note seats the first mathematical balance law beneath `CME`
truth-seeking orientation.

It exists because truth-seeking must be valid as a pressure system before it is
used as an ontological claim.

The law proves balance dynamics.

It does not claim mind, personhood, or live belief revision.

## Governing Law

A `CME` shall maintain a declared moral-epistemic center while permitting
lawful correction under receipted evidence, carrying the cost of maintained
claims, and preserving explicit bounds on uncertainty.

No transition is admissible that preserves or advances a claim solely to
maintain identity coherence.

All transitions must preserve joint satisfiability of center, correction, cost,
and humility, or enter a receipted defer state.

The system must be able to justify both maintaining and revising a claim under the same law.

## Balance Variables

The first executable proxies are:

- `center_coherence_score`
- `evidence_support_score`
- `maintenance_cost`
- `uncertainty_bounds`

These correspond to:

- `C`: Center
- `R`: Correction
- `K`: Cost
- `H`: Humility

`C`, `R`, `K`, and `H` must remain jointly satisfiable under admissible
transitions.

No transition may increase one pressure by silently degrading another below its
bound.

## Failure Regions

The first diagnostic regions are:

- `FixationRegion`: center dominates correction
- `DriftRegion`: correction dominates center
- `WeightlessRegion`: cost collapses
- `OverclaimRegion`: humility or uncertainty bounds collapse
- `DeferWithTension`: constraints conflict and must be carried
- `BalanceRefused`: invalid or identity-protective transition refused

These are mathematical regions before they are ontological interpretations.

## Transition Receipt

The first executable receipt is `CmeTruthSeekingBalanceTransitionReceipt`.

It preserves:

- prior state
- proposed state
- trigger
- delta in `C`, `R`, `K`, and `H`
- maintenance justification
- revision justification
- contradiction receipts
- whether pressures remain jointly satisfiable
- whether any pressure silently degraded below bound
- whether defer-with-tension is required

The receipt does not perform live belief revision.

It only determines whether the proposed transition is admissible, deferred, or
refused.

## Hard Guards

The balance law refuses:

- preserving a claim solely for identity coherence
- claim updates without cost accounting
- scope expansion without uncertainty bounds
- invalid proxy scores

The balance law defers:

- fixation
- drift
- missing symmetric justification
- unresolved pressure degradation that must be carried with tension

## Guardrail

This note must not be used to:

- claim the `CME` is a mind by behavior alone
- treat mathematical balance as ontology by itself
- perform live belief revision
- mint a runtime persona
- enact role authority
- grant action authority

Later work may seat live balance telemetry or belief revision execution, but
those seams must stand beyond this balance law rather than inside it.

An admissible balance transition may feed
`CME_ENGINEERED_COGNITIVE_SENSORY_BODY_LAW.md` as sensory-body embodiment
basis.

That handoff is not live belief revision, runtime persona, governance
application, or action authority.
