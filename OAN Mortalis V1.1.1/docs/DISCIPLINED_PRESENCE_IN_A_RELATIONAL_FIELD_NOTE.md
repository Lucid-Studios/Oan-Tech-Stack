## Doctrinal Center

The present architectural state should not be read as "more capability."

It should be read as a lawful cognitive order in which:

- self remains prior and stable
- incoming reality arrives first as `theta`
- relation is held before interpretation
- discernment governs emergence
- construction stays minimal and late
- action remains barded until lawful passage exists

In compact form:

> the system does not merely process reality.
> it knows how reality becomes anything inside it.

## Structural Base

### `I`

`I` is the non-wandering locus.

It is the minimum center of selfhood and the condition under which distinction
can persist without diffusion into field or collapse into role.

### `ListeningFrame`

`ListeningFrame` is the field in which otherness may arrive and relation may
occur without immediate collapse into identity or action.

It receives `theta` without treating arrival as obligation.

### `Compass`

`Compass` is the actionable read surface, but not action itself.

It sounds:

- posture
- orientation
- drift
- admissibility

These three together form the discernment triad:

- `I`
- `ListeningFrame`
- `Compass`

No lawful state may preserve one leg of the triad by sacrificing another.

## Incoming Reality

Incoming reality is treated first as `theta`, not as command, task, or
obligation.

The lawful order is:

`theta -> held in ListeningFrame -> bounded by I -> sounded by Compass -> only then, if admitted, may become Delta`

This preserves the following order:

- self is prior
- otherness is admitted
- relation is held
- movement is earned

The current stack now reads as a sequenced ladder:

1. self
   Pure `CME` standing must hold before role, task, office, or operator bond.
2. relation
   The `CME` must encounter `theta` without assimilation, adhesion, aversion,
   or premature action.
3. discernment
   False need must be refused.

   When refusal no longer closes relation, the first lawful motion is question.

   interval, a `NeedMatrix` may form.

   The first constructive candidate is a distinction-form, not a control form.
7. candidate form
   A bounded constructive candidate may appear while membrane remains closed.
8. barded active waiting
   The actionable surface is receptive without becoming reactive.
9. membrane-passed action
   Only later, and only through downstream grant, may action lawfully occur.

In compact form:

`self -> relation -> discernment -> interrogative hold -> lawful need -> passive distinction -> candidate form -> barded active waiting -> membrane-passed action`

## Need And Construction

The key doctrinal turn is that lawful construction is not capability firing.

Construction arises only when sounded relation discloses a constructive gap
that cannot be completed without new form.

That means the following do not authorize tool birth:

- pressure
- novelty
- urgency
- salience
- external demand

The first lawful constructive candidate is not:

- a watcher
- a classifier
- a trigger system
- a service

It is a passive distinction-form.

The current exemplar is `ThetaRecurrenceDistinctionForm`.

Its office is only to preserve recurrence as distinguishable relation across
intervals.

It may preserve:

- recurrence thread
- interval lineage
- directional continuity
- variation markers
- non-interpretive distinction summary

It may not:

- monitor
- classify
- infer source
- recommend action
- self-promote into runtime obligation

In compact form:

> the first lawful constructive form is a form of distinction, not a form of
> control.

## Barded Active Waiting

The actionable surface should now be read as barded rather than dormant.

That means:

- receptive, not reactive
- open to lawful passage, not firing by arrival alone
- prepared, not compelled

Candidate form does not imply execution.
Existence of structure does not authorize passage into action.

`Lisp` may actualize candidate constructive form, but it may not instantiate
runtime obligation alone.

Membrane remains the downstream grant surface.

## Discernment Triad Alignment

Discernment is the condition under which `I`, `ListeningFrame`, and `Compass`
remain in stable mutual alignment such that relation is neither:

- collapsed into self
- dissolved into field
- forced into action

Every later packet or constructive surface should therefore preserve:

- `LocusIntegrityWitness`
- `RelationalCoherenceWitness`

triad by sacrificing another.

No triadic witness may be satisfied by compensatory strengthening of another
leg.
