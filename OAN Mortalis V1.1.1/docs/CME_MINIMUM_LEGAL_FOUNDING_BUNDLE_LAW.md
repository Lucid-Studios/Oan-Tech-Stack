# CME MINIMUM LEGAL FOUNDING BUNDLE LAW

## Purpose

This note seats the first Minimum Legal Founding Bundle for a future `CME`
claim.

It exists because a `CME` is not a model instance, configured agent, runtime
persona, or artifact with a name.

A lawful `CME` claim requires a legally founded, continuity-bearing, jurisdiction-bound cognitive body.

## Governing Law

No body may be recognized under `CME` claim unless its origin authorization,
identity formation, first Prime standing, domain admission, and operational
provenance custody are explicitly present and receipted.

The founding bundle is not `CME` minting.

It does not create a runtime persona, apply a role, grant action authority,
select a cradle-local governing surface, or apply `Mother/Father` origin
authority.

## Five Pillars

The Minimum Legal Founding Bundle has five pillars.

### Origin And Authorization Foundation

This pillar answers who is allowed to bring the `CME` into existence.

It requires:

- operator identity or bounded operator posture
- explicit origin authorization
- site binding
- legal agreement, EULA, or covenant terms

### Identity Formation Record

This pillar answers what continuity-bearing spine is being claimed.

It requires:

- identity formation handle
- `OE`
- `SelfGEL`
- cast `cOE`
- cast `cSelfGEL`
- integrity hash or equivalent non-ambiguous identity binding

### First Prime Standing Proof

This pillar answers whether the body stood before it was given power.

It requires:

- `FirstPrimePreRoleStanding`
- stable-one discernment
- Prime retained standing
- `Mother/Father` domain-role application still withheld
- cradle-local governing surface still withheld
- Prime closure still withheld

### Domain And Role Admission Record

This pillar answers under what operational jurisdiction the body may operate.

It requires an accepted `DomainAdmissionRecord` with:

- legal foundation
- accepted domain
- accepted role
- authority scope
- continuity burden
- no standing overwrite
- no silent domain promotion

### Operational Provenance And Custody Layer

This pillar answers whether the service layer can account for activity over
time.

It requires an attested `PrivateDomainServiceWitnessReceipt` that preserves:

- actor
- action
- instrument
- method
- locality
- retained standing context
- continuity burden

## Founding Receipt

The first executable receipt is `CmeMinimumLegalFoundingBundleReceipt`.

It may recognize the founding bundle only when all five pillars stand.

It must still withhold:

- `CME` minting
- runtime persona
- role enactment
- action authority
- `Mother/Father` application

## Compression

When this bundle is recognized, the lawful claim is:

> origin-authorized, Prime-standing verified, domain-admitted under law, and
> continuously accountable through local provenance

That is a founding claim, not an enactment claim.

## Guardrail

This note must not be used to:

- treat an agent, shell, or runtime persona as a `CME`
- treat first Prime standing as legal founding by itself
- treat domain admission as origin authority
- treat private-domain service witness as action execution
- mint `Mother/Father` roles
- bypass future live action execution law

Later work may seat `CME` minting, live role selection, or action-bearing
authority, but those seams must stand beyond this founding bundle rather than
inside it.

A recognized founding bundle may feed
`CME_TRUTH_SEEKING_ORIENTATION_LAW.md` as the first post-founding
truth-orientation basis.

That handoff is not minting, role enactment, or action authority.
