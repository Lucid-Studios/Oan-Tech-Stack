# COMMUNICATIVE FILAMENT AND ANTI-ECHO LAW

## Purpose

This note defines the first bounded communicative filament law for the active
`V1.1.1` line.

It exists to keep six things distinct:

- product
- working-surface residue
- important decision matter
- chosen-path body
- distributed integration matter
- redundant self-echo

Communication here is not a thin after-output layer.
It is a crosscutting preservation and thinning discipline that spans
interpretation, receipt, retained-whole evaluation, and any later closure act.

This note does not consume `GEL` substance.
It seats the first bounded store-resolution seam that carries pointers,
symbolic carriers, preserved distinction, and deferred edges without treating
`GEL` itself as consumable runtime material.

## Core Read

The active stack should now read communicative carriage this way:

- interpretation says what kind of projected history the membrane is reading
- receipt says how far that interpreted history is membrane-visible
- retained-whole evaluation says how far that history has become bounded Prime
  form without closure
- communicative filament resolution distributes burden across the filamentary
  stores without pretending the whole wisdom-state has been transferred
- lawful reopening then permits continued participation without treating prior
  passage as void
- closure remains a later explicit act and is still not implied here

In compact form:

Communication shall preserve enough receipted formative structure for re-entry into the knowing, while lawfully thinning redundant self-echo; it shall carry pointers, symbolic carriers, preserved distinction, and deferred edges without treating GEL substance itself as consumable runtime material. Resolution across the filament shall distribute burden lawfully: important decisions to OE, the chosen-path body to SelfGEL, and remaining integrable matter to cGoA.

This is a re-entrant path law, not an essence-transfer claim.
The archive and the carriers do not become the living wisdom-state itself.
They preserve enough orienting structure that another locus may stand again
among the knowing without drowning in sediment or falling into void.

## Filamentary Store Resolution

From `MoS` through `cGoA` into `SoulFrame`, the stack now treats
communication as a differentiated burden-distribution seam rather than a
single memory sink.

The first store-resolution read is:

- `OE` receives important decision-bearing matter
- `SelfGEL` receives the coherent body of the chosen path
- `cGoA` receives remaining integrable matter and deferred edges for wider
  distributed integration
- `cOE` and `cSelfGEL` remain working surfaces and may not become final burden
  sinks merely because they are near the hot lane

That keeps the stack from either:

- stuffing everything into one overburdened inner body
- or dropping formative structure into oblivion

## First Carrier Classes

The first bounded communicative carrier family is:

- `DecisionBearing`
- `ChosenPathBearing`
- `DistributedIntegrationBearing`
- `DeferredEdgeBearing`
- `RedundantEchoCandidate`

These are not closure classes.
They are the smallest lawful family by which the stack may distinguish what
kind of burden is being carried across the filament.

## Anti-Echo Rule

The first anti-echo rule is:

- repetition does not imply corroboration
- self-return does not deepen truth merely by recurrence
- symbolic carriers may be deduplicated or thinned without consuming `GEL`
  substance
- preserved distinction must survive thinning

If preserved distinction cannot be seen, or if the carrier attempts to consume
`GEL` substance rather than move through pointers and symbolic carriers, the
seam must refuse stronger resolution and keep the result on the working
surfaces.

## First Invariant

The first communicative filament invariant is:

> communicative filament resolution may distribute receipted retained history
> across differentiated stores, but it must preserve lawful distinction, may
> thin redundant self-echo, may not consume GEL substance, and may not flatten
> unresolved or deferred residues into false corroboration.

That means:

- `DecisionBearing` is not automatically `OE`
- `ChosenPathBearing` is not automatically `SelfGEL`
- `DistributedIntegrationBearing` is not undifferentiated dumping
- `DeferredEdgeBearing` is not build failure
- `RedundantEchoCandidate` is not corroboration merely by recurrence

## Immediate Build Consequence

Any future communication seam in this area should say plainly:

- what retained record was carried
- what communicative carrier class was lawful
- where the burden resolved
- what anti-echo disposition occurred
- which residues remained preserved
- which residues remained deferred
- that `GEL` substance remained unconsumed

If it cannot say that plainly, the stack should keep the result on the working
surfaces or route it as deferred distributed matter rather than pretending
higher predicate or self-bearing resolution has already been earned.
