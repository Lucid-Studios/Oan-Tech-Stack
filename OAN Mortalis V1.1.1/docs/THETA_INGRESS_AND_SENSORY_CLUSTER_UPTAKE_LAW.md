## Status Set

The first bounded intake status set is:

- `Lawful`
- `Deferred`
- `Blocked`
- `Malformed`

Meaning:

- `Lawful`
  - the thread was present in `ListeningFrame`, crossed relative to `Zed of Delta`,
    was taken at `cOE`, entered `cSelfGEL`, and contextualization lawfully began
- `Deferred`
  - the traversal leg stands, but review posture or reviewable self-basis
    prevents stronger uptake claims
- `Blocked`
  - the traversal cannot complete lawfully because field, basis, or ingest leg
    is not intact
- `Malformed`
  - the thread itself was not lawfully named enough to witness ingress
