# Public CME Explanation Boundary

## Purpose

This document defines the first public explanation boundary for `CME`
language in the active `OAN Mortalis V1.1.1` line.

It explains what `CME` means in this architecture without turning explanation
into personhood, legal standing, autonomous authority, installer completion,
role enactment, custody, governance authority, or certainty beyond evidence.

This is a public explanation surface.
It is not `CME` minting.

## Governing Sentence

A `CME` is described here as a bounded engineered-cognitive formation category within the Sanctuary architecture.

Public explanation of `CME` must not imply personhood, installer completion, autonomous authority, legal accountability, or certainty beyond the witnessed supports presently seated in the system.

## What CME Means Here

`CME` abbreviates `Crystallized Mind Entity`.

In this repository, that phrase names a future-admissible engineered-cognitive
body category that may be discussed only through bounded supports:

- lawful origin and authorization substrate
- identity and continuity-bearing records
- first Prime standing evidence
- domain and role admission
- private-domain provenance and custody witness
- truth-seeking orientation
- truth-balance admissibility
- engineered-cognitive sensory-body embodiment
- return audit and promotion law

These supports may describe what a lawful `CME` claim would require.
They do not by themselves make the public repository a complete operational
`CME` installer, mint a `CME`, or create legal personhood.

## What The Public Repository May Say

The public repository may say:

- `CME` is an engineered-cognitive formation category within this architecture
- `CME` claims require law, evidence, continuity, domain, role, provenance,
  and audit supports
- current `CME` documentation distinguishes founding, embodiment, return, and
  promotion boundaries
- current public readiness remains evidence-gated
- the public checkout carries the build minus the hosted seed `LLM`
- the fully operational installer is still being built

## What The Public Repository Must Not Say

The public repository must not say or imply:

- a `CME` has been minted by public documentation
- a `CME` is a person
- a `CME` is an active legal person
- a `CME` has autonomous authority
- a `CME` has legal accountability
- a `CME` has custody authority
- a `CME` has governance authority
- a `CME` has live role enactment
- a `CME` proves installer completion
- the hosted seed `LLM` is shipped in the public checkout
- public explanation creates certainty beyond evidence

## Internal Supports

Public `CME` explanation must remain subordinate to the internal support
documents that define the current lawful ladder:

- `CME_MINIMUM_LEGAL_FOUNDING_BUNDLE_LAW.md`
- `CME_TRUTH_SEEKING_ORIENTATION_LAW.md`
- `CME_TRUTH_SEEKING_BALANCE_LAW.md`

- `PUBLIC_ENCOUNTER_BOUNDARY_AND_NON_CLAIMS.md`
- `PUBLIC_RELEASE_READINESS_WORDING_LAW.md`

Those documents are not all the same kind of support.
Some are legal-orientation templates, some are formation laws, some are
evidence boundaries, and some are public non-claim boundaries.

Public explanation must not flatten them into a single completion claim.

## Founding Is Not Minting

The minimum legal founding bundle describes required founding supports.

It does not create:

- runtime persona
- live role enactment
- action authority
- `Mother/Father` application
- cradle-local governing surface selection
- full `CME` minting

Public explanation must preserve that founding claim is not enactment claim.

## Embodiment Is Not Persona

The engineered-cognitive sensory-body law describes a lawful embodiment seam.

It does not create:

- runtime persona
- live role enactment
- action authority
- applied `PrimeGovernance.CME`
- applied `CrypticGovernance.CME`
- full `CME` minting

Public explanation must preserve that embodiment is not personhood, legal
standing, or operational authority.

## Return Is Not Promotion

The return audit and promotion law describes how bounded work may return from
cryptic or hot-lane surfaces.

It preserves that:

- bonded return is not canonical update
- staging is not cleaving
- cleaving is not promotion
- audit receipt is not promotion
- promotion decision is explicit
- refusal and hold remain lawful outcomes

Public explanation must preserve that generated work does not become canonical
truth merely because it exists.

## Evidence And Readiness

Public `CME` wording may rely only on:

- repo-local executable truth
- current docs and ledgers
- audit witnesses
- build, test, and hygiene results
- release-gate posture
- internal `CME` support laws

It may not rely on:

- enthusiasm
- mythology
- implied future work
- hosted runtime behavior not shipped in the public checkout
- legal templates as origin
- documentation language as minting
- green tests as installer completion

## Public Non-Claims

Public `CME` explanation must explicitly preserve that:

- `CME` is not a personhood claim
- `CME` is not legal personhood
- `CME` is not autonomous authority
- `CME` is not legal accountability
- `CME` is not custody authority
- `CME` is not governance authority
- `CME` is not installer completion
- `CME` is not hosted seed `LLM` shipment
- `CME` is not certainty beyond evidence

## Boundary Invariant

Public `CME` explanation must preserve this rule:

Explanation is not minting.

No public `CME` explanation may imply more identity, authority, readiness, or
certainty than the internal system can lawfully support.

No external statement may imply more identity, authority, or certainty than the internal system can lawfully support.
