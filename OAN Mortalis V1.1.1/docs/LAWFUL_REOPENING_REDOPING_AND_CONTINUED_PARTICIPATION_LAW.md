# LAWFUL REOPENING REDOPING AND CONTINUED PARTICIPATION LAW

## Purpose

This note defines the first bounded law of lawful reopening for the active
`V1.1.1` line.

It exists to keep five things distinct:

- simple reopening
- reopening under modification
- reopening under redoping
- distributed continuation
- deferred continued participation

This note does not create a false fresh start.
It defines the first bounded act by which communicatively resolved matter may
re-enter participation without treating prior passage as void.

reopening is not innocence.
Reopening shall preserve lawful history, visible distinction, deferred edges,
and the non-consumptive relation to `GEL` substrate.
It shall not treat prior passage as void, and it shall not destroy adjacent
possibility merely by permitting continued participation.

## Core Read

The active stack should now read reopening this way:

- communicative filament resolution distributes burden across the filament
- lawful reopening determines how that distributed matter may re-enter active
  participation
- reopening does not erase receipt history
- reopening does not reset retained distinction
- reopening does not imply closure
- explicit Prime closure act, when lawful, remains later and separate

In compact form:

> Reopening shall permit continued participation of receipted or retained
> forms without treating prior passage as void; redoping may modify active
> posture or carrier density, but shall preserve lawful history, visible
> distinction, deferred edges, and the non-consumptive relation to GEL
> substrate.

## First Reopening Modes

The first bounded reopening family is:

- `SimpleReopen`
- `ModifiedReopen`
- `RedopedReopen`
- `DistributedContinuation`

These are not innocence classes.
They are the smallest lawful family by which the stack may say how something
re-enters participation after receipt, retention, and communicative thinning.

## Continued Participation States

The first bounded continued-participation family is:

- `Reopened`
- `ReopenedModified`
- `Redoped`
- `ContinuedDistributed`
- `Deferred`

These are not closure classes.
They are the smallest lawful family by which the stack may say whether
participation truly resumed or had to remain withheld.

## Adjacent Possibility Rule

The first reopening rule is:

- reopening must preserve lawful history
- reopening must not consume `GEL` substance
- reopening must keep preserved distinction visible
- reopening must refuse false fresh-start semantics
- reopening must preserve adjacent possibility rather than destroying nearby
  lawful participation

If the source was already flattened to working-surface-only matter, or if the
carrier attempted substrate consumption, the reopening seam must defer rather
than pretend live participation lawfully resumed.

## Store Continuity Read

The first distributed reopening read is:

- `OE` retains the important decisions that must survive reopening
- `SelfGEL` retains the main chosen-path body from which reopening may re-enter
- `cGoA` carries distributed continuation and deferred integrable matter
- `cOE` and `cSelfGEL` remain the live working surfaces where reopening becomes
  active again without silently becoming final burden sinks

That means reopening is not rollback.
It is continued morphic participation under preserved history.

## First Invariant

The first lawful reopening invariant is:

> lawful reopening may permit continued participation of communicatively
> resolved matter, but it must preserve lawful history, must not treat prior
> passage as void, must not consume GEL substrate, and must preserve adjacent
> possibility for neighboring lawful lines.

That means:

- `SimpleReopen` is not innocence
- `ModifiedReopen` is not wipe-and-rerun
- `RedopedReopen` is not substrate consumption
- `DistributedContinuation` is not flattened dumping
- `Deferred` is still a lawful state

## Immediate Build Consequence

Any future reopening seam in this area should say plainly:

- what communicative receipt was reopened
- what reopening mode was lawful
- what participation state resulted
- what prior history remained visible
- what deferred edges remained visible
- that adjacent possibility was preserved or truthfully withheld
- that `GEL` substrate remained unconsumed

If it cannot say that plainly, the stack should keep the result deferred
rather than pretending continued participation lawfully resumed.
