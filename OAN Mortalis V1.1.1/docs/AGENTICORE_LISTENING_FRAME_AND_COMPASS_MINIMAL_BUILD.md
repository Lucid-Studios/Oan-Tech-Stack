# AGENTICORE LISTENING FRAME AND COMPASS MINIMAL BUILD

## Purpose

This note defines the smallest lawful build slice that lets `AgentiCore`
operate with a real `Listening Frame` and `Compass` seam without pretending
that final governance authority has moved into cognition.

It exists to keep five things distinct:

- content or substrate field
- posture field
- orientation and admissibility read
- bounded coordination inside `AgentiCore`
- supervising runtime authority outside `AgentiCore`

This is a minimal-build note.
It is not a full `EC` habitation claim and not a continuity or persistence
promotion note.

## Core Read

The cohesive operational chain is:

`content/substrate field -> Listening Frame projection -> Compass orientation and admissibility work -> AgentiCore coordination -> host-side authority decision`

That chain matters because it prevents a common collapse:

- content is not posture
- posture is not judgment
- judgment is not authority

## Definitions

### Listening Frame

`Listening Frame` is the auditable posture surface through which live cognition
becomes legible before stronger action, correction, or persistence is allowed.

It is not passive reception.
It is the bounded field in which signal is held long enough to be oriented,
compared, and lawfully judged.

In compact form:

> `Listening Frame` is the governed semantic posture field in which drift
> becomes reviewable and lawful movement can begin without premature
> commitment.

### Compass

`Compass` is the orientation, drift, and admissibility read performed over the
`Listening Frame`.

It is not another memory bank and not just another learned slice of the
content field.

Its office is narrower:

- situate posture
- estimate drift
- estimate orientation
- estimate admissibility
- recommend bounded transition posture

In compact form:

> `Compass` is orthogonal to the content field in governance function, not
> detached from that field in observational basis.

### AgentiCore

`AgentiCore` is the bounded coordination layer that may consume
`Listening Frame` and `Compass` outputs during live mission work.

Its office here is:

- propose
- coordinate
- pause
- route
- emit receipts

It does not own final admission, correction authority, persistence, or
continuity law.

## Minimal Build Rule

The minimal build is not a sovereign `Compass` module living inside
`AgentiCore`.

It is the smallest posture layer that lets `AgentiCore` use `Compass` as an
auditable governance overlay over substrate-shaped cognition rather than as
another dataset-shaped memory object.

The minimal build must provide exactly enough structure for:

1. projection into a `Listening Frame`
2. drift and threshold observation
3. lawful transition review
4. orientation and admissibility scoring
5. candidate-only handoff into higher authority

Everything beyond that is widening, not minimum viability.

## Guardrail

This layer is diagnostic and coordinative.
It is not sovereign.

That means:

- shell harmonics
- resonant-form work
- repair chambers
- drift bands
- modulation and regime signals

may enter here as candidate calibration or repair surfaces.

They may help the runtime:

- situate cognition
- classify regime
- test stability
- propose correction
- shape lawful variation

They may not by themselves decide:

- persistence
- continuity admission
- final governance outcome
- durable carry-forward
- engram eligibility

In compact form:

> candidate and diagnostic tool states may become operationally usable inside
> `AgentiCore`, while final authority remains outside that layer.

## Family Placement

  posture layer into the line

- making `Listening Frame` into an unbounded memory or identity surface
