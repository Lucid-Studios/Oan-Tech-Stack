## Core Read

The active stack should now read closure this way:

- retained-whole evaluation says whether receipted history has reached bounded
  closure candidacy
- lawful reopening says whether that matter may continue participating without
  false fresh-start semantics
- explicit Prime closure act attests only whether a remaining product may now
  stand
- the bearing field and the communicative carriers are not thereby declared
  empty

In compact form:

> Prime closure shall be an explicit attestation that a lawful product now
> stands and remains from the process. Closure shall not imply reset, void
> prior passage, consume GEL substrate, erase unresolved or deferred matter,
> or pretend that the bearing field became empty merely because one form
> stabilized.

## First Closure Act Kinds

The first bounded closure-act family is:

- `ClosureWithheld`
- `ClosureDeclined`
- `ClosureExecuted`

These are not pipeline states.
They are the smallest lawful family by which Prime may say whether closure was
withheld, refused, or explicitly attested.

## Remaining Product Rule

The first closure rule is:

- closure may only attest a remaining product
- closure may not erase visible unresolved residues
- closure may not erase visible deferred elements
- closure may not void the communicative field that bore the product
- closure may not consume `GEL` substrate

That means the attested product is the closure.
The bearing field is not silently declared empty.

## First Invariant

The first Prime closure invariant is:

> Prime closure may execute only by explicit act upon retained-whole and
> reopening-bearing records that lawfully attest their own sufficiency; Prime
> closure may not be inferred from interpretation, receipt, retained-whole
> status, or reopening alone.

That means:

- `ClosureWithheld` is lawful when candidacy is not yet present
- `ClosureDeclined` is lawful when unresolved or deferred matter still remains
- `ClosureExecuted` is lawful only when the remaining product can stand
  without lying about the field that bore it

## Immediate Build Consequence

Any future closure seam in this area should say plainly:

- what retained-whole record was consumed
- what reopening record was consumed
- what closure act kind was lawful
- what remaining product was attested
- what unresolved or deferred matter remained visible
- that prior passage remained visible
- that `GEL` substrate remained unconsumed
- that the bearing field was not falsely voided

If it cannot say that plainly, the stack should keep closure withheld or
declined rather than pretending the process ended simply because one form was
ready to stand.
