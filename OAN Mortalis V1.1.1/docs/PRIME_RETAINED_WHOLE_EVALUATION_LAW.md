## Core Read

The active stack should now read Prime-side retained-whole evaluation this
way:

- interpretation says what kind of projected history Prime is reading
- receipt says how far that interpreted history is membrane-visible
- retained-whole evaluation says whether receipted history is admitted into
  bounded retained form
- communicative filament resolution then distributes the retained burden across
  `OE`, `SelfGEL`, and `cGoA` without consuming `GEL` substance
- closure remains a later explicit act and is not implied here

In compact form:

> Prime-side retained-whole evaluation may admit receipted history into
> retained form to a bounded degree, but retained-whole shall not imply
> closure unless a later explicit closure act lawfully attests that all
> required distinctions, residues, and deferred elements have been resolved or
> intentionally bounded.

## First Retained Whole Kinds

The first bounded retained-whole family is:

- `NotRetained`
- `RetainedPartial`
- `RetainedWholeUnclosed`
- `ClosureCandidate`
- `StillDeferred`

These are not closure classes.
They are the smallest lawful family by which Prime may distinguish receipt
from retained form without collapsing them into one act.

## Retained And Unresolved Residue Rule

Prime-side retained-whole evaluation must answer two residue questions
explicitly:

- which residues have been admitted into retained form
- which residues remain unresolved or deferred beyond this evaluation

This preserves that retained form may lawfully deepen while still withholding
closure.

## First Invariant

The first retained-whole invariant is:

> Prime-side retained-whole evaluation may admit receipted history into
> bounded retained form, but it must preserve lawful distinction, may not
> silently erase unresolved residue, and may not treat retained-whole as Prime
> closure.

That means:

- `NotRetained` is not build failure
- `RetainedPartial` is not retained whole
- `RetainedWholeUnclosed` is still not closure
- `ClosureCandidate` is still not closure
- `StillDeferred` is still a lawful state

## Immediate Build Consequence

Any future retained-whole seam in this area should say plainly:

- what receipted history was evaluated
- what retained-whole kind was lawful
- which residues were retained
- which residues remained unresolved
- that explicit closure still remained required

If it cannot say that plainly, Prime should keep the result as
`NotRetained` or `StillDeferred` rather than pretending retained whole or
closure has already been earned.
